Wait for duration

sleep

[seconds (number)]

Wait for a number of seconds before triggering the next command. Seconds are scaled to the current bpm setting.

Introduced in v2.0 

Example 0 



play 50 
play 55
play 62

sleep 1 

play 50 
sleep 0.5
play 55
sleep 0.5
play 62
